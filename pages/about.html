About Tixbro

Our Story

How we started revolutionizing event ticketing in India

Founded in 2024, Tixbro was born from a simple idea: make event ticketing accessible, reliable, and enjoyable for everyone in India. We saw the challenges people faced when trying to book tickets for their favorite events - long queues, sold-out shows, and complicated booking processes. We knew there had to be a better way.

Today, Tixbro has grown into India's most trusted ticket booking platform, serving millions of happy customers across 200+ cities. From concerts and sports events to college fests and turf bookings, we've made it our mission to connect people with the experiences they love.
