## üì± General Guidelines

### Color Palette
- Incorporate Indian colors: Saffron (#FF9933), White (#FFFFFF), Green (#138808)
- Use vibrant, warm colors that appeal to Indian audience
- Ensure good contrast for text readability

### Cultural Considerations
- Show diversity: Different ages, regions, and backgrounds
- Avoid stereotypes: Show modern India alongside traditional elements
- Include both urban and semi-urban settings
- Show technology adoption (smartphones, online booking)

### Technical Specifications
- **Format:** JPEG for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency
- **Size:** Optimize images (use tools like TinyPNG)
  - Carousel: 1920x1080px, <150KB
  - Service cards: 800x600px, <80KB
  - Office/City: 600x400px, <60KB
  - Testimonials: 200x200px, <20KB (circular crop)

## üìù Implementation Steps

1. **Download/Purchase Images**
   - Follow recommendations above
   - Ensure proper licensing for commercial use

2. **Optimize Images**
   - Use tools: TinyPNG, ImageOptim, or Squoosh
   - Target: <150KB for large images, <50KB for thumbnails

3. **Replace Files**
   - Keep same filenames OR update HTML references
   - Test on different devices (mobile, tablet, desktop)

4. **Update Alt Text**
   - Add descriptive alt text for SEO and accessibility
   - Example: `alt="Young Indians booking movie tickets on Tixbro"`

5. **Test Performance**
   - Check page load speed (Google PageSpeed Insights)
   - Ensure images load properly on slow connections

## üí° Pro Tips

- **Use WebP format** for better compression (with JPEG fallback)
